EVENTS



CORE TALKS

Outreach Cell brings you the solution for some career-related doubts; “CORE TALKS”- a series of talks to meet the most distinguished alumni, pioneering in your own department, and guide you through the mist of your confusion. Be it core or non-core, whatever calls your interests, research or corporate or maybe something totally out of the league, this is your chance to explore it all. Know about the opportunities, and learn about the challenges.



TIPS FROM THE TOP

'Tips from the Top,’ where you get to know and talk directly with the adept alumni of our college. Every alumnus was once a student sitting in another such session with some alumni. One day we will pass out and face life itself. What will guide you then are the trails left by our alumni.



MOCK-en-JOY

Outreach Cell brings you the opportunity of facing an IITK Alumnus for a Mock Interview through the first-of-its-kind event "Mock-en-Joy." A lot of times, the fear of facing the interviewer overpowers the interviewee's confidence. Mock interviews give us a chance to reflect on our shortcomings and strengths. One must step out of his/ her comfort zone and gain the confidence needed for facing the interviews.Keeping this in mind,outreach cell keeps on collaborating with IITK Alums.
